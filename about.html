About CYSKY

CYSKY is a cutting-edge offensive security research group specializing in identifying vulnerabilities in web and cloud applications. We emphasize thorough manual testing, responsible disclosure, and continuous improvement for our clients.

Our Mission

To provide startups and SaaS businesses with unparalleled security insights, combining automated and manual methods, and helping them defend their digital assets.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced security researchers with deep industry knowledge.
  • Proven track record on Bug Bounty platforms and real-world projects.
  • Dedicated to responsible disclosure and client success.
  • Fast response times and detailed actionable reports.
